Trekker's Home

Ever since I started trekking in 1995 it has become my overwelming passion. I dont trek all that much, but whenever I am not trekking I am dreaming of trekking. These pages are the essence of my experiences till now. I have put them up in the hope that others may also enjoy what I have. And in enjoying they may protect it for our future.

abhijit
